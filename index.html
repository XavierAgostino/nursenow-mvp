Got questions? We've got answers

Everything you need to know about NurseNow

How quickly can shifts really be filled?

Our average fill time is 2.3 hours, with urgent shifts often filled in under 60 minutes. Our instant push notification system ensures qualified nurses see your posting immediately.

What's included in nurse verification?

Every nurse undergoes license verification, background checks, skills assessment, reference checks, and insurance verification. We maintain ongoing compliance monitoring.

How does payment processing work?

Nurses submit timesheets through the app, facilities approve, and payment is processed automatically. Nurses receive payment within 24 hours, facilities are billed weekly.

What about liability and insurance?

All nurses carry professional liability insurance. NurseNow provides additional platform coverage up to $5M per occurrence, giving you complete peace of mind.

Can I request specific nurses?

Absolutely! Build your preferred nurse list and send shifts directly to your favorites. Many facilities develop ongoing relationships with nurses they trust.

Is there a minimum commitment?

No minimums, no contracts. Use NurseNow when you need it. Start with a single shift or staff your entire facility - you're in complete control.
